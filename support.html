Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

The cashier shows you a price. Your job is to pay the exact amount using Thai coins and bills. Tap the denominations to toss them on the counter, then hit CHECK to confirm.

What is Challenge mode?

In Challenge mode, the price is only spoken in Thai — it's not displayed on screen. You have to listen carefully and pay the right amount.

How do I get a PERFECT score?

Use the fewest coins and bills possible to pay the exact amount. For example, pay 50 baht with one 50฿ bill instead of five 10฿ coins.

The app is crashing or not loading

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, make sure your device is running iOS 15 or later and that you have the latest version of JaaiTang! installed.
